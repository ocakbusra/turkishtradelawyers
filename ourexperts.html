Büşra Ocak

Co-Founder & Managing Partner

Büşra Ocak, as the third generation of a family business in trade and manufacturing, gained early entrepreneurial exposure before pursuing law. She specializes in contract law, GDPR/KVKK compliance, employment disputes, and commercial litigation.

Büşra has provided comprehensive legal counsel to leading companies across e-commerce, textile, energy, telecommunications, and education sectors. Her expertise includes drafting and auditing commercial agreements, managing enforcement and debt collection proceedings, implementing data protection frameworks, and completing VERBİS registration processes for corporate clients.

She has successfully handled complex contractual disputes and preventive legal strategies for businesses navigating regulatory challenges.

LL.B. Faculty of Law, Ankara University
Certified by Turkish Competition & Energy Authorities
Union of Turkish Bar Associations & Istanbul Bar Association

Başak Çavuşoğulları

Co-Founder & Senior Partner

Başak Çavuşoğulları advises European companies operating in Turkey on commercial law, regulatory compliance, and cross-border transactions particularly in manufacturing, construction, and machinery industries.

Başak also provides legal consultancy to Turkish companies on commercial matters, handles medical malpractice and aesthetic procedure litigation, and leads the firm's immigration and corporate services practice, guiding foreign nationals through company formation, work permits, and citizenship applications. With a background in international arbitration from her academic years at Istanbul University Faculty of Law, she brings a dispute resolution perspective to complex commercial matters.

LL.B. Faculty of Law, Istanbul University
Member of Young ITOTAM
Union of Turkish Bar Associations & Bursa Bar Association
