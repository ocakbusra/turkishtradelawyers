Real Estate and Commercial Lease Services in Turkey

We support companies taking, transferring, or restructuring commercial premises in Turkey, with a focus on lease risk, negotiation strategy, title issues, and property-rights protection.

Scope of Real Estate Support

  • Commercial lease drafting, review, and negotiation for office, warehouse, and retail premises
  • Rent adjustment and termination-risk planning
  • Property transfer support and rights analysis from a business-use perspective
  • Alignment of lease obligations with operational and corporate requirements
  • Document review where commercial occupation and real-estate rights intersect

Why This Matters to Businesses Entering Turkey

Real-estate decisions often lock in operational risk. A lease or transfer issue can affect staffing, licensing, logistics, and capital planning, so legal review needs to be integrated with the commercial rollout.
