What We Handle

Collection matters in Turkey often require speed, documentary discipline, and the right procedural route from day one. We structure the file around enforceability, leverage, and practical recovery options.

  • Pre-action demand letters, payment negotiations, and settlement structuring
  • Enforcement proceedings without judgment and objection management
  • Provisional attachment strategy where asset dissipation is a concern
  • Support for invoices, supply agreements, guarantees, and cross-border receivables documentation
  • Coordination with foreign counsel when the underlying transaction spans multiple jurisdictions

Why This Matters for Foreign Creditors

Delayed action can weaken leverage. In Turkey, timing, evidentiary quality, and procedural choice can directly affect whether funds are recovered quickly, disputed for months, or rendered commercially uncollectable.

  • We evaluate whether the matter is better approached through amicable recovery, enforcement, or a court-backed route
  • We identify pressure points such as payment history, guarantees, shipment records, and acknowledgment of debt
  • We help structure communication so that commercial negotiations do not unintentionally prejudice your legal position
