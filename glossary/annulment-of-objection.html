Annulment of Objection

İtirazın İptali

Dispute Resolution

Definition

A lawsuit filed by a creditor after a debtor formally objects to and halts an execution proceeding without judgment, seeking to annul the objection and resume execution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a penalty for unjust objections?

Yes, if the creditor proves the debt, the court may order the debtor to pay an execution denial compensation of at least 20% of the debt.
