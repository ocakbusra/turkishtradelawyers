Assignment

Temlik

Contract Law

Definition

The legal transfer of a right, property, or claim from one party (assignor) to another (assignee), frequently used for transferring receivables.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does assignment require the debtor's consent?

No, assignment of receivables generally does not require the debtor's consent, but the debtor must be notified to make payment to the new creditor.
