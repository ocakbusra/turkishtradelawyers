Our Specialized Data Protection Services

We provide targeted compliance solutions tailored to Turkish regulations for both local and international companies.

Data Controller Representative (DCR) Services

Acting as your appointed local representative in Turkey to manage relations with the KVKK Authority, handle data subject requests, and fulfill your legal representation obligations as a foreign entity.

VERBİS Registration

End-to-end management of the Data Controllers Registry (VERBİS) registration process, including data inventory preparation, category mapping, and official system filings.

Privacy & Cookie Policy Compliance

Drafting and localizing Privacy Notices, Explicit Consent Forms, and Cookie Policies to ensure full compliance with Turkish KVKK and e-commerce legislation.
