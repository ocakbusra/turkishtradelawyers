Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work fully remotely with international clients?

Yes, we work seamlessly with clients worldwide through video conferences, secure document sharing, and responsive communication. While based in Istanbul, we serve clients across Europe, the Americas, and Asia.

How fast can you review a contract?

Standard contract reviews take 3-5 business days. For urgent matters, we offer expedited review within 24-48 hours. Complex transactions may require additional time, which we communicate upfront.

Can you coordinate with our headquarters' counsel?

Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with in-house legal teams and external counsel in clients' home countries. Our multilingual team ensures seamless coordination across jurisdictions.

Do you provide fixed-fee options?

Yes, we offer fixed-fee arrangements for defined-scope matters such as company formation, contract drafting, and compliance reviews. For ongoing advisory work, we provide flexible monthly retainer packages.

Which matters require notarization or Power of Attorney?

Company formation, real estate transactions, court representation, and official filings typically require notarized documents and Power of Attorney. We guide you through the apostille process for documents from abroad.

Do you support arbitration and enforcement?

Yes, we handle institutional arbitration (ISTAC, ICC) and ad-hoc proceedings. We also manage enforcement of foreign arbitral awards under the New York Convention and recognition of foreign court judgments.
